This game is set where advantage is lost by referencing the rules of the game. 
Here the rules are visible to all parties at all time. What changes is the verification system. 
---We aim to prove that an event follows all the rules without disclosing the event. 
---But specially we aim to prove that an event breaks one or multiple rules without telling which ones, nor the event. 

When freedom is very serious, you want to be able to use the law without referecing it, because 
referencing the law puts the law in great danger of being modified. 

The following artifcat shows how to build a jury that can impart a decition guarantee to follow the law 
without specifying the chapter and articles. It separates legislation from judicary power. 

Think about it as, a jury who orders "x because of hash(y)" instead of "x bacause of y" ---and so to protect "y".
