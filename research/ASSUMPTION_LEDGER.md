# Assumption Ledger

## Purpose

This ledger makes explicit the trust, identity, network, randomness,
cryptographic, implementation, and institutional assumptions on which current
manuscript ideas depend. An assumption marked **implicit** is an audit inference
required for the claim to make sense; it is not language already approved by
the author. The RPS, Hash Functions, and repaired Trust entries are explicit in
their active chapters. Trust rows `AS-TRUST-01` through `AS-TRUST-12` preserve
the former draft's assumptions and risks; rows beginning at `AS-TRUST-13`
describe the active model. Tic-Tac-Toe rows `AS-TTT-01` and `AS-TTT-02`
preserve the fragment's implicit assumptions; the later TTT rows describe the
bounded public-checker model without promoting it into a private protocol.
