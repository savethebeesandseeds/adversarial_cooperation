# Chapter Matrix

## Reading Key

- **Included** describes the live include graph rooted at
  `document/adversarial_cooperation.tex`, not the intended book order.
- A **candidate** baseline, primitive, or threat model is an audit inference,
  not text already established by the manuscript.
- **Novelty remains unreviewed unless a row explicitly says otherwise.** The
  RPS slice now has a narrow prior-art check sufficient to classify its
  commit--reveal pattern as known; the project still makes no novelty claim for
  that chapter or its exact hash-based construction.
- Priority: **P1** foundation or first vertical slice; **P2** next technical
  layer; **P3** later formalization; **P4** hold until the common language is
  mature.
